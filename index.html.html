How it works

One app, three people making it move.

A customer orders. A shop owner prepares it. A rider carries it across the neighborhood. LocalLine is the thread between all three.

🛍️
For customers

Order from the shops you already know

Browse by category, track your rider live, and get exactly what your local store had in stock.

  • Real-time order tracking on a map
  • Category-first browsing, no clutter
  • Support chat, right inside the app
🏪
For shop owners

Sell what's already on your shelf

List your catalog, accept orders as they arrive, and reach customers on your own street.

  • Simple product & stock management
  • Daily payouts, tracked automatically
  • No commission surprises
🛵
For riders

Earn on routes that make sense

Get offered nearby deliveries first, choose your shifts, and see exactly what you'll earn.

  • Flexible full-time or part-time shifts
  • Minimum guaranteed pay on qualifying days
  • Transparent, itemized daily earnings
